👩‍⚕️ Nurse Assistant Training Program (NATP)

Launch your healthcare career with our state-approved NATP course designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills to become a Certified Nurse Assistant (CNA).

📅 Study Options:

  • Weekday Classes: Monday – Thursday
  • Weekend Classes: Friday and Saturday

🗓 Current Sessions

  • Weekday Class (1439): June 16 – July 10, 2025
  • Weekend Class (1440): June 27 – August 16, 2025

✅ Entry Requirements

Applicants must meet one of the following:

  • High school diploma or GED (evidence required)
  • Pass the Combined English Language Skills Assessment (ATB Test)
  • Completion of 6 credit hours or 225 clock hours toward a recognized degree or certificate
